I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in Suresh Jagannathan's group at the Purdue University. I completed my Ph.D. at Regal, a joint research group of INRIA and University Pierre and Marie Curie (UPMC, Paris 6), under the supervision of Marc Shapiro , in May 2016. My expertise is in a range of areas in distributed systems and parallel programming. The main theme of my work is developing and analyzing distributed systems such as cloud computing, along with associated tools, to help programmers safely design and develop programs intended to run in a failure-prone large-scale replicated distributed environments. My work targets domains such as concurrent and distributing programming, complex data consistency models, program debugging and program verification. .
